Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

VoiceForge AI ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account and use VoiceForge AI, we may collect:

  • Account information: your name, email address, and password (stored securely as a hashed value, never in plain text).
  • Usage data: the text you submit for voice generation, selected voices, and generation history, so you can access your past projects.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and device information collected automatically for security and analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the VoiceForge AI platform.
  • Generate the audio you request using our text-to-speech engine.
  • Save your generation history so you can revisit previous projects.
  • Communicate with you about your account, updates, or support requests.
  • Improve our services, detect misuse, and maintain platform security.

3. How We Protect Your Data

Passwords are hashed using industry-standard encryption before being stored. We use secure, authenticated connections to our database, and access to user data is restricted to what's necessary to operate the service.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted third-party services strictly necessary to operate VoiceForge AI, such as our voice synthesis provider, and only to the extent required to generate your requested audio.

5. Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your account information at any time from your Settings page. You may also contact us to request a copy of your data or full account deletion.

6. Cookies

We use minimal local storage on your device (such as your login session) to keep you signed in. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

7. Children's Privacy

VoiceForge AI is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Support page.
