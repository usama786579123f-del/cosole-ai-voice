Frequently Asked Questions

How do I generate my first voice?

▾

Sign up for a free account, go to "Text to Speech" from your dashboard, type your text, pick a voice, and click Generate Speech.

Can I use generated audio commercially?

▾

Yes, on Starter plan and above you get full commercial usage rights for all generated audio.

How do I reset my password?

▾

Go to Settings → Security tab, and set a new password. You can also use "Forgot password" on the login page.

Do you offer refunds?

▾

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans if you're not fully satisfied.

How do I contact enterprise sales?

▾

Fill out the contact form here selecting "General Inquiry" and mention Enterprise — our team will reach out within a day.
